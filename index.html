Achievements

Bringing ICSE syllabus
Bringin Icse syllabus

Vikaasa was the first school to bring ICSE syllabus to Tamil Nadu when it was affiliated to CISCE as TN001 in the 70's. It again became the first to bring international syllabus to Madurai by becoming the first school to be affiliated to Cambridge university offering IGCSE and A-levels.

What's new

  • Vikaasa plans to build a new campus in Pudhoor

    Vikaas school has a lot of branches like Vikaasa progressive school(both 1 and 2) in Achampathu,Vikaasa Heritage Campus in ponagaram,Vikaasa World School in Oomachikulam and now it is planning to build a new branch in Pudhoor making the school a very big school.

  • Student scores in 10th board exam

    In Vikaasa School, out of 182 students, 152 scored marks with distinction in ICSE exams, while 58 out of 82 scored marks with distinction in ISC. “The top scores in ICSE was 97.5% while for ISC it was 95.6%. A total of 40 centums were scored in various subjects by ICSE students while five were scored by ISC students,” said Navaneetha Krishnan, principal, Vikaasa School.
